Who we are

May creates digital healthcare products on mobile, wearables, and the web. May is owned and run by partners in San Francisco and Adelaide who saw the opportunity to transform healthcare delivery through the intelligent application of design and technology. We combine our expertise in design, software development, and medical practice to create amazing experiences for practitioners, patients, and caregivers.

What we believe

As devices and the networks on which they communicate become faster, more integrated, and accessible, the ability to make an impact on the quality of and access to healthcare increases exponentially. We believe that the products we make have the potential to improve quality of life and have a meaningful impact on millions of people, and we work closely with our clients to deliver world-class applications.

How we work

We practice human-centered design – we start with the people, and end with solutions tailored to their needs. Our products are always informed by research, user testing, and clinical practice. We take a holistic approach, applying the same level of quality and craftsmanship to every part of the product. We prototype, test, and iterate quickly, creating a tight feedback loop to minimize risk and increase your product’s success.
